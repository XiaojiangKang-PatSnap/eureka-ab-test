FAQ Section

What is FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliance?
FDA 21 CFR Part 11 establishes criteria for electronic records and electronic signatures to be considered trustworthy, reliable, and equivalent to paper records and handwritten signatures.
How does Eureka LS ensure data integrity?
Our platform includes comprehensive audit trails, role-based access control, and data validation checks to ensure complete data integrity throughout your research workflow.
Can Eureka LS integrate with our existing systems?
Yes, we offer REST API access and pre-built integrations with major LIMS, ELN, and ERP systems commonly used in pharmaceutical research.
